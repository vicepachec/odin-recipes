Guy Dinner

Even though the name says guy dinner anyone can partake in trying this recipe do not worry

Sometimes life just gets to much to the point where you don't even have the strength to cook anything so why not try some guy dinner

Ingredients

  • The loneliest dinner for one you can find
  • A calm spot where you can look at the night sky
  • A longing for change

Steps

  1. Buy the dinner for one from you're nearest store
  2. Find a calm spot where you can contemplate all of your existence from
  3. Start eating it and let the thoughts take over, think of how life got you like this, what you want to achieve, what sacrifices you are willing to make
  4. Stare at the stars and hope that their shine can bring some clarity in your life
  5. Enjoy the silence
